Why Selis?

The Challenge

All ministries, government agencies, hospitals, and major sectors have clear strategies, but the real challenge isn't creating the strategy—it's executing it and turning it into visible results on the ground.

The Solution: Selis

The national platform that reconnects planning with execution, transforming strategy into measurable, trackable action. We link strategy with initiatives, programs, and projects within a unified platform.

Ajlan & Bros Holding Group

One of the largest private investment conglomerates in the Middle East

Ajlan & Bros Holding Group — one of the largest private investment conglomerates in the Middle East.

What Selis Platform Delivered
  • 1. Implementation of an Investment Opportunity Management System, enabling the client to register and manage all potential clients through the conversion cycle to an investment opportunity leading to final signing.
  • 2. Empowering strategic management and project management offices with integrated platforms for project management and strategic progress tracking on one unified platform.
  • 3. Supporting this empowerment with customized dashboards for the Group's CEO.

Saudi Government Authority

Tasked with attracting investment through economic cities and special economic zones

A Saudi government authority tasked with attracting investments to the Kingdom through economic cities and special economic zones.

What Selis Platform Delivered
  • 1. Implementation of a Project Management Information System (PMIS) across all core operational sectors of the Authority.
  • 2. Implementation of a digital procurement tool within the same platform.
  • 3. Using Selis to track digital transformation measurement indicators and report recommendations to the Digital Governance Authority (DGA).

Saudi Government Ministry

Tasked with enhancing GDP contribution and industrial transformation

A Saudi government ministry tasked with enhancing GDP contribution to transform the Kingdom into a leading industrial and mining power, and a global logistics hub.

What Selis Platform Delivered
  • 1. Implementation of a Compliance Management Information System (CMIS) to enable the ministry to manage and track multiple compliance indicators with defined workflows.
  • 2. Internal application created a collaborative work environment between departments, reducing many manual tasks in following up with external entities to provide compliance evidence.
